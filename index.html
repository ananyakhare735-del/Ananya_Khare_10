Overview

The BMW i8 is a revolutionary plug-in hybrid sports car that combines futuristic styling with sustainable performance. It pairs a 1.5-litre turbo petrol engine with an electric motor to deliver instant torque and thrilling acceleration, while lightweight carbon fiber construction and aerodynamic design keep handling razor-sharp. Iconic scissor-style doors, premium interiors and advanced infotainment make the i8 both a head-turner and a technological statement.

Features

Hybrid Powertrain

1.5L turbo petrol + electric motor delivering strong combined output, instant torque and improved efficiency.

Performance

0–100 km/h in ~4.4s (dependent on variant), top speeds electronically limited; agile chassis and low centre of gravity.

Lightweight Tech

Carbon-Fiber Reinforced Plastic (CFRP) and aluminium construction for superior stiffness and reduced mass.

Electric Range

Pure electric driving range for city trips (around 25–35 km in real-world usage depending on conditions).

Design & Comfort

Futuristic cabin, driver-focused infotainment, climate control, premium materials and iconic butterfly doors.

Safety

Multiple airbags, stability & traction control systems, advanced driver aids and reinforced occupant cell.

Models

The BMW i8 was offered primarily in two versions: the i8 Coupe and the i8 Roadster. Both share the same hybrid drivetrain but offer distinct experiences — the Coupe for pure sporty dynamics and the Roadster for an open-top driving experience.
